📘 About This Application

This is a study aid for the Dubai Corporation for Ambulance Services (DCAS) Clinical Practice Guidelines 2025. It is designed to help EMS personnel review guidelines through summaries, flashcards, quizzes, and critical scenarios.

Important Notice This application is for educational purposes only. It does not replace the official DCAS Clinical Practice Guidelines or any official memos. Always refer to the latest official DCAS documents and follow your scope of practice and local protocols.

Features

Four themes (Dark, Light, Sepia, Forest)
Completion tracking for chapters
Progress dashboard with statistics
Flashcards with 3D flip
Quizzes with adjustable questions
Critical scenarios with KPIs
Full‑text search functionality

How to Use

  • 🎨
    Theme Toggle: Click the palette icon to cycle through four color themes.
  • 📋
    Index Button: Opens the full table of contents for quick navigation.
  • 📊
    Stats Badge: Shows your total quiz attempts and scenario accuracy.
  • 🔍
    Search Bar: Type keywords to filter chapter cards in real time.
  • ✅
    Mark Done: Track your progress manually on cards.
