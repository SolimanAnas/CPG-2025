About This Application

DCAS Clinical Practice Guidelines 2025

This is a comprehensive study aid designed for the Dubai Corporation for Ambulance Services (DCAS). It assists EMS personnel in reviewing Clinical Practice Guidelines through interactive summaries, 3D flashcards, quizzes, and critical scenarios.

The application is built to be intuitive and fast, allowing for quick reference and study sessions on the go. It features progress tracking to help you visualize your learning journey across all chapters.

Important Notice This application is for educational purposes only. It does not replace the official DCAS Clinical Practice Guidelines or any official memos. Always refer to the latest official DCAS documents and follow your scope of practice and local protocols.

Author

Created by Soliman Anas Soliman, an Emergency Medical Technician dedicated to improving educational accessibility for EMS professionals.

Key Features

  • Four color themes (Dark/Light/Sepia)
  • Real-time Progress Dashboard
  • Interactive 3D Flashcards
  • KPI-based Critical Scenarios
  • Full-text Instant Search
  • Chapter Completion Tracking
