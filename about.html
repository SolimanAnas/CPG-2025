About This Application

This is a dedicated study aid for the Dubai Corporation for Ambulance Services (DCAS) Clinical Practice Guidelines 2025. It is designed to assist EMS personnel in mastering guidelines through interactive summaries, 3D flashcards, quizzes, and critical scenario simulations.

Important Notice This application is for educational purposes only. It does not replace the official DCAS Clinical Practice Guidelines or any official memos. Always refer to the latest official DCAS documents and follow your scope of practice and local protocols.

Author

Created by Soliman Anas Soliman
Emergency Medical Technician

The goal of this project is to provide an accessible, mobile-friendly platform for continuous learning and quick reference in the field.

Features

  • Personalized Themes: Toggle between Dark, Light, Sepia, and Forest modes.
  • Progress Tracking: Dashboard statistics for quizzes and scenarios.
  • Study Tools: Flip-flashcards and adjustable quiz lengths.
  • Smart Search: Full-text search across the main menu and index.

How to Use

  • Use the Palette Icon (🎨) in the header to switch themes.
  • Tap the Stats Badge to view your learning analytics.
  • Use the Search Bar on the home screen to filter chapters instantly.
